ABOUT SATVIKK E-COMMERCE PRIVATE LIMITED

Satvikk E-Commerce Private Limited is a Bangalore-based start-up snacking dry fruit brand led by women entrepreneurs, specializing in premium dry fruit products. We prioritize sustainability by using eco-friendly paper packaging. With over 500 products, including dry fruits, chocolates, vegan items, and more, Satvikk offers a wide variety.

Our team comprises more than 50% women, and we are currently appointing distributors nationwide. Our motto is simple: quality production of vegan and vegetarian products, ensuring maximum benefits with minimum investment.

We are a bootstrapped company with ISO certification and hold relevant food certifications. Not only this, but we are also a digital-friendly company, with an e-commerce platform, and an active member of the BSDA.

Our commitment to quality and sustainability makes us a trusted choice in the dry fruit industry.
